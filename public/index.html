Recent Form and Head-to-Head Analysis

Arsenal enter this match in superb domestic form, having lost just once in their last 15 Premier League matches. Their attacking fluency has been particularly impressive, with the front three combining for 35 goals this season. The Gunners have scored at least two goals in their last eight home matches across all competitions.

Atletico Madrid have been typically solid in La Liga, remaining unbeaten in their last 8 matches. Their European experience under Simeone makes them a dangerous opponent in knockout football, with the manager having reached two Champions League finals. Atletico have kept clean sheets in five of their last seven away games in European competition.

Historically, matches between these sides have been tight affairs. Their last meeting in European competition ended in a 1-1 draw, with both teams showing respect for each other's qualities. Arsenal have won just one of their five previous encounters with Spanish opposition at the Emirates Stadium.
